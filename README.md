# Facebook Ads Performance Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Facebook advertising campaign data with the objective of evaluating campaign effectiveness, audience engagement, conversion performance, and return on investment (ROI).  
An interactive Power BI dashboard is developed to convert raw marketing data into strategic insights that support data-driven decision-making for digital marketing optimization.

---

## Business Objectives
- Evaluate overall Facebook ad campaign performance using key marketing metrics  
- Measure engagement efficiency through CTR and conversion analysis  
- Analyze advertising spend in relation to revenue and ROI  
- Compare campaign performance across demographic segments and interest groups  
- Enable identification of high-performing and underperforming campaigns  

---

## Tools and Technologies
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive dashboard development  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data extraction and preprocessing  
- **Marketing Analytics** – CTR, CPC, conversion rate, ROI analysis  
- **Data Visualization** – KPI design, trend analysis, comparative insights  

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains structured Facebook advertising data at the campaign and ad level, including:

- Campaign and ad identifiers  
- Campaign start and end dates  
- Audience demographics (age group, gender)  
- Interest-based targeting attributes  
- Impressions, clicks, and advertising spend  
- Conversion and approval metrics  

---

## Data Preparation and Modeling
Prior to visualization, the dataset was cleaned and modeled to ensure analytical accuracy:

- Standardized date fields and numeric measures  
- Removed inconsistent and invalid records  
- Applied appropriate data types for all columns  
- Created calculated measures using DAX for KPIs such as CTR, cost per conversion, and ROI  

---

## Key Performance Indicators
The dashboard highlights the following business-critical KPIs:

- Total Advertising Spend  
- Total Impressions  
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)  
- Total Conversions  
- Cost per Conversion  
- Revenue versus Spend  
- Return on Investment (ROI)  

---

## Dashboard Capabilities
- High-level KPI monitoring through summary cards  
- Campaign-wise CTR and engagement comparison  
- Spend versus ROI performance evaluation  
- Time-based trend analysis of spend and clicks  
- Interactive filters for age group, gender, campaign ID, and interests  

---

## Insights and Outcomes
- Identified campaigns delivering higher engagement and ROI  
- Highlighted inefficient spend areas requiring optimization  
- Revealed audience segments contributing most to conversions  
- Enabled performance tracking over time for informed campaign decisions  

---

## Business Impact
This dashboard supports digital marketing teams in:
- Optimizing ad budgets and targeting strategies  
- Improving campaign efficiency and ROI  
- Monitoring performance trends in real time  
- Making informed, data-backed marketing decisions
